    GRAMMAR TIPS
    • 'H' is silent
    • 'Chau' (Chao) = informal 'adios'
    • Mexicans > 'Latinos'
    • SUBJECTS:
      1. I = Yo
      2. You = Tu/Yusted/Vos
      3. He = El
      4. She = Ella
      5. It = Esto (masc); Esta(fem)
      6. We = Nosotros (masc); Nosotras(fem)
      7. They = Ellos (masc); Ellas(fem)
    • 'El' y 'la' = 'the' (masc/fem nouns)
    • 'Mucho gusto' = more informal 'nice to meet you'
    • 'Bien' = 'good'
    • 'No entiendo' more common than 'no lo comprendo' (w/o 'lo' > more personal)
    • Upside down question mark (¿) is a standard punctuation mark in Spanish. It alerts that the sentence is a question before they begin to read.
    • Bailou = BAY-llow(lou)
